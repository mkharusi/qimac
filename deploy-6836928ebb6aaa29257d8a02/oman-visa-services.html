Who It's For:

Companies hiring expatriate staff for postings in Oman (all sectors & skill levels).

Documents You Supply (Scan/PDF):

  • Passport (≥ 6 months validity)
  • Digital photo (white background)
  • Academic / professional certificates (for regulated roles)
  • Signed employment contract
  • Employer's Commercial Registration (CR) & specimen-signature sheet

What We Do:

  1. Role validation & labour clearance (Ministry of Labour).
  2. Employment contract drafting in English & Arabic.
  3. e-Visa application on the Royal Oman Police (ROP) portal.
  4. Medical-fitness appointment & results upload.
  5. Biometric capture & Residence Card issuance on arrival.
  6. Optional: airport meet-and-greet, accommodation check-in, family-visa bundling.

Key Benefits:

  • Single point of contact
  • Arabic & English support
  • Government liaison
  • Medical booking
  • Fingerprints & card collection
  • Clear upfront cost: OMR 195 all-in
  • No hidden extras
  • Fast-track option (5-7 days)
  • Family-visa add-on available
  • Status updates by email, WhatsApp, or client portal

    • Investment & Timeline:

    Government Fee: OMR 20 per two-year work visa. This is payable directly to the Royal Oman Police (ROP). For more details, visit rop.gov.om or omanepotal.com.

    Our Comprehensive Service Fee: OMR 175 per applicant. This is a flat fee covering all liaison, translations, and submissions.

    Total Package Investment: OMR 195 (Government Fee + Our Service Fee).

    Volume Discounts: We offer attractive discounts for 10+ visa applications. Please inquire for a custom quote.

    Typical Timeline: The end-to-end process typically takes 7 – 21 calendar days, contingent upon prompt delivery of all required documents. For insights into employee mobility, you might find playroll.com a useful resource.
