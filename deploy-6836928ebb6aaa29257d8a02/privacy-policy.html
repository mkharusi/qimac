2. Cookie Policy

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track the activity on our website and hold certain information. Cookies are files with a small amount of data which may include an anonymous unique identifier. Cookies are sent to your browser from a website and stored on your device.

2.1 Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable basic functions like page navigation and access to secure areas of the website. The website cannot function properly without these cookies.

  • Purpose: Website functionality and security
  • Duration: Session or up to 365 days
  • Cannot be disabled: These cookies are mandatory

Analytics Cookies

We use Google Analytics cookies to understand how visitors interact with our website. These cookies help us improve our website's performance and user experience.

  • Provider: Google Analytics (ID: G-1TY61TJCKZ)
  • Purpose: Website analytics and performance monitoring
  • Data collected: Page views, time on site, user behavior, device information
  • Duration: Up to 2 years
  • Can be disabled: Yes, through our cookie consent banner

Marketing Cookies

These cookies are used to track visitors across websites. The intention is to display ads that are relevant and engaging for individual users.

  • Purpose: Marketing and advertising
  • Data collected: User preferences, ad interaction data
  • Duration: Up to 1 year
  • Can be disabled: Yes, through our cookie consent banner

2.2 How to Control Cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. You can delete all cookies that are already on your computer and you can set most browsers to prevent them from being placed. However, if you do this, you may have to manually adjust some preferences every time you visit our website and some services and functionalities may not work.

Cookie Management Options:

  • Use our cookie consent banner to manage your preferences
  • Adjust your browser settings to refuse cookies
  • Use browser extensions to manage cookies
  • Clear your browser cookies regularly
